"Make your own nature, not the advice of others, your guide in life"

Take three deep breaths.

Consider your life for a moment.
All of the Chaos; all of the Order.
The routines, people, setbacks, surprises.
Think of the JOY. Think of the SORROW.
Recall certain events.
Perhaps from ages ago or just moments earlier.
Even ones that may yet come to pass.
Once your thoughts are focused,
shuffle the deck and draw your cards.
